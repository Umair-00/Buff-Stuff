Privacy Policy

Buff Stuff - Workout Tracker

Last updated: January 2026

Summary: Buff Stuff stores all your workout data locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

Data Storage

All workout data you enter into Buff Stuff is stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Workout sessions and history
  • Exercise library and custom exercises
  • Sets, reps, and weight records
  • Notes and change requests

This data never leaves your device unless you choose to sync it with Apple Health.

Apple Health Integration

If you enable HealthKit integration, Buff Stuff can:

  • Write completed workout sessions to Apple Health
  • This includes workout duration, start time, and end time
  • Detailed exercise data (sets, reps, weights) is NOT sent to Apple Health

You can enable or disable this feature at any time in the app's Settings tab. Apple Health data is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

Data Collection

Buff Stuff does not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Any data transmitted to external servers

Third-Party Services

Buff Stuff does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by:

  • Deleting individual workouts within the app
  • Uninstalling the app (removes all app data)

Children's Privacy

Buff Stuff does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new version on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
